Mein apne dil k sab sachaion ke saath ye iqrar karta hun

      mein tum se pyaar karta hun 
             
            magar jo keh raha hun main mumkin hai
 pure sachai ki aanch ussey na guzri ho

 mere hoton pe jo lafz e mohabbat hai
 Bohot mumkin ho wo meri jarurat ho 

Adhuri chahatein meri adhuri dastan meri 
  Mere jazbe adhorey hein
  Mere khwahis k pemane adhurey hein 
  Muhabbat pe mere hoton ke afsane adhore hein
Adhurey pan k dunya mere charon taraf hai
  
Fir bhi apne dil k sab gehrayon k saath sab sachaion ke saath mein ekraar karta hun

mein tumse pyaar karta hun JAAN
